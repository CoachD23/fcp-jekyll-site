| Gap | Why FCP Loses | Fix |

| "basketball training academy" | No dedicated training methodology page | Create `/basketball-training-academy/` highlighting Spartan Training Center |

### Content Gaps vs. Each Competitor

**vs. DME Academy:**
- DME has TWO post-grad team pages (Black + National/Blue) — FCP has one
- DME has facility-specific content (NBA courts, shooting lab, video analysis)
- DME has a Wikipedia page — FCP does not
- DME has coaching hire announcements as SEO content — FCP does not

**vs. IMG Academy:**
- IMG has massive content depth (100+ articles, news, events)
- IMG has "boarding school" positioning baked into URL structure
- IMG has alumni success stories with real names (Anfernee Simons, etc.)
- IMG has event pages that generate backlinks (Sunshine Prep Showcase)

**vs. Academy of Central Florida:**
- ACF has MaxPreps integration for multiple teams
- ACF has the Orlando metro geographic advantage
- ACF claims 30+ NBA, 175+ D1 placements — larger proof points

**vs. prepathletics.com (the real SEO threat):**
- PREP Athletics has comprehensive guide content ("What is post-grad basketball?")
- They rank #1 for informational intent queries through pure content quality
- They frame the entire category — anyone searching learns about post-grad through THEIR lens
- FCP needs to become the authoritative SOURCE, not just a listing in their directory
