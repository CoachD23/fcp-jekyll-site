How FCP Gets Athletes Recruited

1

Individual Recruiting Assessment

Athletes begin with a one-on-one evaluation — position, skill set, academic standing, and eligibility timeline — then get a realistic recruiting target list built across D1, D2, D3, NAIA, and JUCO programs that match their profile.

2

Game Film & Recruiting Packages

Games and training sessions are filmed at the Spartan Training Center. Coaches help athletes identify and present the footage that best represents their game to programs at the right level.

3

National Tournament & Circuit Exposure

FCP competes on SEHAL, PHSBA, Grind Session, and SIAA — circuits with active college coach attendance. FCP also hosts the National Prep Tournament in Fort Walton Beach, which draws programs from across the country.

4

Direct Coach Outreach

FCP coaches make direct calls and send film to specific programs. We leverage active relationships at every level — not blast emails. Your name gets in front of the right coaches at the right time.

5

NCAA Eligibility & Compliance

Our staff has navigated NCAA Eligibility Center clearance, NAIA eligibility, and transfer portal rules hundreds of times. Athletes leave FCP academically eligible and scholarship-ready.

Supervised Housing on the Emerald Coast

FCP athletes live in structured, supervised housing in Fort Walton Beach — steps from the Gulf of Mexico. A dedicated house coach lives on-site and enforces curfew, room checks, and daily schedules. The environment mirrors college program accountability.

Housing is subject to availability. Contact us for current openings.

Location & Training Facility

Spartan Training Center

A professional-level facility with full NBA/FIBA courts, modern strength & conditioning equipment, and film study capabilities. Athletes train daily in a structured environment designed to replicate the demands of college basketball. See the facility →

Fort Walton Beach, Florida

On the Emerald Coast of the Florida Panhandle — a safe, focused environment for student-athletes with world-class beaches a few minutes away. No winter shutdowns. FCP runs a full training calendar 12 months a year.

✈️ Destin-Fort Walton Beach Airport (VPS) — 5 min
✈️ Pensacola International (PNS) — 45 min

Year-Round Florida Weather

Outdoor conditioning in December. Beach workouts in February. The climate is a competitive advantage — FCP athletes train harder, longer, and more consistently than programs in cold-weather states.
