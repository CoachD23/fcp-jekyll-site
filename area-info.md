Okaloosa Island is situated in Northwest Florida on the Florida Panhandle. The area centered around Fort Walton Beach offers diverse attractions and recreational opportunities.

## Area Attractions

The region features numerous attractions including:

- The Gulf Breeze Zoo
- Gulf Islands National Seashore
- Indian Temple Mound Museum
- Santa Rosa Mall
- Adventures Unlimited Canoeing
- Henderson Beach State Park
- Destin's Fishing Fleet
- National Museum of Naval Aviation
- Gulfarium and the Living Sea
- Blackwater River State Park
- The Emerald Coast Science Center
