Who Thrives at FCP?

The Undiscovered Talent

Skilled players who didn't get the right exposure in high school. FCP's national schedule puts you in front of college coaches who need to see you play.

The Academic Rebuilder

Athletes who need to strengthen GPA or core course requirements to become NCAA/NAIA eligible. We have pathways that work.

The Late Bloomer

Players still filling out physically and developing their game. An extra year at FCP often results in scholarship offers that weren't on the table before.

The Level-Up Seeker

Players with D1 or high-major potential who need one more year of elite competition to earn the scholarship they deserve.

Academics

For Post-Grad Athletes

Online dual-enrollment through accredited partner colleges. Earn real college credits while competing, recover core courses for eligibility, and graduate with transferable academic progress toward a degree.

Academic counselors work alongside athletes to manage NCAA/NAIA eligibility timelines and ensure every credit counts.

For High School Athletes

Fully accredited diploma program designed around the athlete's schedule. Small class sizes, flexible scheduling, and academic coaches who understand that basketball IS the priority — not a distraction.

Graduates leave FCP with an accredited diploma and a recruiting file ready for any collegiate program.

Housing

FCP offers supervised, athlete-focused housing steps from the Gulf Coast in Fort Walton Beach, Florida. Athletes live together in a structured environment that mirrors the accountability culture of a college program — curfews, study hours, meal planning, and team bonding built in.

  • Supervised residency with house parents / program staff oversight
  • Fully furnished rooms — no need to ship furniture
  • Chef-prepared meals or kitchen-equipped units (varies by unit)
  • Walking distance to beach — decompression built into the lifestyle
  • Wi-Fi, utilities, and laundry included
  • Roommate matching by program and grade level

Location

Florida Coastal Prep is located in Fort Walton Beach, Florida — on the Emerald Coast of the Florida Panhandle. The city offers a safe, focused environment for student-athletes with the motivational backdrop of world-class beaches.

Nearest airports:
Destin-Fort Walton Beach Airport (VPS) — 5 min
Pensacola International Airport (PNS) — 45 min
Northwest Florida Beaches International (ECP) — 55 min

Year-Round Florida Weather

No winter shutdowns. No snow days. FCP runs a full training calendar 12 months a year — outdoor sprints in December, beach conditioning in February. The climate is a competitive advantage.
