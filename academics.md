At Florida Coastal Prep, academics are just as important as athletics in shaping the future success of our student-athletes. We have partnered with Lighthouse Private Christian School to deliver comprehensive academic preparation.

## Academic Offerings

**Test Preparation:**
- ACT/SAT Prep classes included in tuition
- TOEFL preparation for international students (separate fee applies)

**Academic Structure:**
- Dual enrollment opportunities with college courses
- Block-style learning format providing flexibility
- High school diploma program through Lighthouse Christian Academy
- Dual credit courses and AP offerings
- NCAA-approved coursework

Our program features challenging, accredited, and NCAA-approved core classes designed for college preparation.

## NCAA Requirements

All student athletes must provide high school transcripts, SAT/ACT test scores to verify NCAA eligibility, and must register with the NCAA Clearinghouse.
