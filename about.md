  <p>Florida Coastal Prep Sports Academy is a premier post-graduate and high school basketball program on Florida's Emerald Coast. We bridge the gap between high school and college athletics — combining elite-level coaching, rigorous academics, and D1-caliber facilities to prepare student-athletes for the next chapter.</p>
