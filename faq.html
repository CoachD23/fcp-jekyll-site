FAQ & Parent Resource Center

Answers to commonly asked questions about Florida Coastal Prep

Enrollment & Admissions

Our High School Program is for student-athletes in grades 9-12 (typically ages 14-18). Our Post Graduate Program is designed for recent high school graduates ages 18-20 who want an extra year to develop before college.

Visit our Apply Now page and fill out the application form. Our coaching staff will review your application, film, and transcripts, then reach out to discuss your goals and next steps. You can also call us at 850-961-2323.

Our program typically runs from August through May (exact dates may vary by year). This includes pre-season training, the competitive season with up to 30 games, and post-season showcases.

Yes, roster spots are limited to ensure each player receives personalized coaching and development. We recommend applying early to secure your spot.

Academics

Yes. All core classes are accredited and NCAA approved. Our academic partner, Lighthouse Christian Academy, provides a high school diploma program that includes dual credit courses, AP offerings, and coursework that meets NCAA Eligibility Center requirements.

Yes, ACT/SAT preparation classes are included in tuition. We also offer TOEFL preparation for international students (separate fee applies).

Yes. We offer dual enrollment opportunities through NW Florida State College, allowing students to earn college credits while completing their high school coursework.

Housing & Daily Life

We offer several housing options including beach condos along the Emerald Coast, a multi-level beach house with a private pool, team houses with full kitchens and lounge areas, and El Matador resort apartments with pool and fitness center access. All options include a house coach for safety and supervision. Learn more on our Housing page.

Absolutely. Our house coach is always present to ensure all housing arrangements are safe and secure. The safety and well-being of our student-athletes is our top priority.

Student-athletes follow a structured, college-like schedule that includes 2 on-court practices/workouts per day, 3 strength and conditioning sessions per week, academic coursework, film study, and beach workouts. Our regimented schedule is designed to optimize player improvement and performance.

Basketball & Recruiting

Our experienced coaching staff has extensive networks of college coaching connections. We provide highlight tapes, game film through Hudl, professional photos, and leverage our schedule against nationally ranked programs to get our players in front of college recruiters. Every game is recorded and uploaded to Hudl and YouTube.

We play a national schedule against the best prep programs, college developmental programs, and junior colleges. This provides our players with the high-level competition and exposure needed to attract college recruiters from NCAA D1, D2, NAIA, and JUCO programs.

The Spartan Training Center is our 14,000 sq ft facility located in Fort Walton Beach, FL. It features a full NBA-dimension hardwood court (94 ft x 50 ft), a 60 ft artificial turf training zone, video board, shot clocks, shooting machines, and a sports performance area. It is available 24/7 for our athletes. Learn more.
