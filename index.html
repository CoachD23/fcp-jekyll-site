About The Academy

Built for the Next Level

Florida Coastal Prep Sports Academy is a high school and post-graduate opportunity for players seeking to earn a college scholarship. Located in Fort Walton Beach, Florida, we partner with Lighthouse Christian Academy to offer NCAA-approved coursework, AP courses, and dual credit options.

Our experienced team of former college and professional coaches — including NBA legend Kenny Anderson — brings an extensive network of connections and college coaching experience. Every player gets a customized development plan designed to make them college-ready on day one.
