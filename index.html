Florida Coastal Prep Sports Academy

Florida Coastal Prep Sports Academy, located in Fort Walton Beach, Florida, is a high school and post-graduate opportunity for players seeking to earn a college scholarship to pay for their academic degree.

Florida Coastal Prep Sports Academy has partnered with Lighthouse Christian Academy to offer a high school diploma that features dual credit courses and AP courses along with NCAA approved coursework. This program gives students an added level of flexibility and convenience when it comes to your choice of education. Engaging lesson plans, 24/7 access, and experienced, certified LCA teachers working with your student will bring schooling to a new level. All core classes are accredited and NCAA approved.

Our housing and facilities are world-class offering the best in training and beach living. In addition, we compete against nationally ranked prep programs, college developmental programs and junior colleges affording our players national exposure and recruiting opportunity.

Florida Coastal Prep is driven by our experienced team of former college and professional coaches. Every coach on our staff has an extensive network of connections along with college coaching experience. Our management and coaching staff is dedicated to providing training and skill development that will prepare our players to be college ready on day 1.

Our Programs

Florida Coastal Prep has a plan for each player and a highly regimented schedule designed to optimize our players improvement and performance.

Post Graduate Program

For graduates seeking an extra year to develop their game, improve academics, and secure a college scholarship. Ages 18-20.

National High School Program

Our high school program offers NCAA approved coursework along with AP classes and dual credit courses with national basketball exposure.
