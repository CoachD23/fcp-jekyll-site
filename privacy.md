## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 2026**

Florida Coastal Prep Sports Academy ("FCP," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or interact with our programs.

## Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

- Fill out a contact or application form
- Subscribe to our email communications
- Contact us by phone, email, or social media
- Visit our website (via cookies and analytics)

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, athlete information (age, grade, position), and academic records.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Respond to inquiries and applications
- Communicate about our programs and opportunities
- Process enrollments and manage student-athlete records
- Improve our website and user experience
- Send occasional updates and news (with your consent)

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our website and programs, subject to confidentiality agreements.

## Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and third-party analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.
