See What Life Is Really Like at FCP.

Florida Coastal Prep produced its own original docuseries — "6 Months to Change Everything" — following our student-athletes through a full season of elite training, national competition, and personal growth. With hundreds of thousands of views, the series gives recruits and families an authentic inside look at the FCP experience.

Inside the Academy

What the Series Covers

Daily Training

Two-a-day practices, individual skill development, film study, and strength and conditioning inside our 14,000 sq ft Spartan Training Center.

Game Day

Behind-the-scenes access to SEHAL and Grind Session matchups — the intensity, the pressure, and the competition that defines a Spartan season.

Brotherhood

The bonds athletes build living, training, and competing together — team dinners, late-night conversations, and the moments that make FCP a family.

The Recruiting Journey

Real stories of athletes working toward college offers — the grind, the setbacks, and the breakthroughs that lead to commitments.
