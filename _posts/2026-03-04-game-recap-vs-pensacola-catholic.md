The FCP Spartans hit the road to face Pensacola Catholic in tournament action, cruising to a **78-65** win behind a balanced effort.

**Team:** Post-Grad Gold

## Notable Performances

| Player | Stats |
|--------|-------|
| Jaylen Moore | 22 pts, 8 reb, 4 ast |
| Marcus Thompson | 18 pts, 3-5 from three |

## Game Highlights

FCP jumped out to a 20-8 lead in the first quarter and never looked back. The defense forced 15 turnovers while the offense shot 52% from the field. Third straight tournament win for the Spartans.
