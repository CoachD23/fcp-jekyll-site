Florida Coastal Prep is proud to announce that forward **Brock Rose** has officially committed to **Radford University**, an NCAA Division I program competing in the Big South Conference. 🎉

Brock put in the work every single day on the Gulf Coast of Florida, and that commitment has earned him a Division I opportunity at a program with a proud tradition. The Radford Highlanders compete in one of the most competitive mid-major conferences in the country, and Brock is ready for that challenge.

## From FCP to the Big South

Radford University, located in Radford, Virginia, competes in the **Big South Conference** — a respected NCAA D1 league that has produced numerous NBA players and professional athletes over the years. Brock will be stepping into a high-level program that will push him to grow as a player and as a student-athlete.

At FCP, Brock developed his game in a professional environment under the guidance of our coaching staff, sharpening the skills and habits that Division I coaches look for. His commitment to Radford is a testament to what daily work, discipline, and the right environment can produce.

## Another D1 Placement for FCP

Brock's commitment continues a strong tradition of D1 placements from Florida Coastal Prep. Our post-graduate program has placed athletes at programs across every level of college basketball — including DePaul, Troy University, Northern Michigan, and now Radford.

> "Brock earned this. He came in locked in and never let up. Radford is getting a skilled, high-character player who is ready to compete at the D1 level." — FCP Coaching Staff

## Congratulations, Brock! 🏀

The entire Florida Coastal Prep family is behind you. We can't wait to watch you compete at the Division I level.
