Florida Coastal Prep recently hosted basketball legend Kenny Anderson for an impactful visit with our Post Grad Players. Anderson, a former NBA point guard who played for teams including the New Jersey Nets, Boston Celtics, and Charlotte Hornets, brought invaluable experience and wisdom to the academy.

## Anderson's Basketball Pedigree

Before his professional career, Anderson was a standout at Georgia Tech, where he earned All-American honors and led the Yellow Jackets to NCAA Tournament appearances. His NBA journey showcased exceptional ball-handling skills and court vision throughout his career spanning multiple franchises.

## Drills and Skill Development

During his visit, Anderson led the Post Grad squad through comprehensive training sessions emphasizing ball handling, shooting techniques, and defensive footwork. He shared signature moves developed throughout his career, making each drill both challenging and engaging for the players.

## Motivational Message

Anderson emphasized the importance of resilience and perseverance. His key message to players was straightforward: "Be good people, and everything will work out." He stressed becoming a "Pro In Life," dedicating oneself not just to basketball but to personal character development.

## Life Lessons and Inspiration

Anderson openly discussed his journey, including struggles and setbacks. He emphasized the value of humility and giving back to the community, demonstrating that true success involves mentoring younger athletes and lifting others up.

Anderson plans to return to work with Florida Coastal Prep's high school basketball program, continuing to inspire the next generation of athletes.
