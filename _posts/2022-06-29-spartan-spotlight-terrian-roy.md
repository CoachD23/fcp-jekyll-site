This feature highlights Terrian Roy, a Class of 2021 guard standing 6'1" from Ville Platte, Louisiana. Roy was an important player during his time at Florida Coastal Prep.

Coach Dmitry Utolin praised Roy's dedication: "Terrian was all about the process of improving from day 1 and never hesitated in his commitment to the team and the program. The pandemic tested all of us, but it never fazed TRoy and his laser focus on reaching the highest level possible."

## Q&A with Terrian Roy

**Advice for incoming post-grad players:** Roy recommends newcomers concentrate on hard work and fundamental basketball skills, emphasizing that mental focus and eliminating distractions are essential for advancement.

**Favorite memory:** Roy treasured the strong relationships developed with coaches and teammates, noting that team chemistry helps everyone achieve their potential.

**What he'd do differently:** He would have invested more intensity in strength training, recognizing its importance for college-level play.

**Why FCP:** Roy selected Florida Coastal Prep to showcase his abilities, calling it his finest decision and crediting the program with helping him reach unexpected heights.
