Kenny Anderson's basketball journey began at Georgia Tech, where he was a standout player before being drafted by the New Jersey Nets in the first round of the 1991 NBA Draft. Over his 14-year career in the NBA, Anderson played for several teams, showcasing his exceptional talent and leadership. He earned a reputation as one of the league's premier point guards, known for his agility, vision, and ability to control the game.

At Florida Coastal Prep, we couldn't be more excited to welcome Anderson. His addition to our coaching staff signifies our commitment to providing top-tier training and development for our athletes. Anderson's experience at the highest levels of basketball will be invaluable in mentoring our players and helping them reach their full potential.

Head Coach Lee DeForest stated: "His expertise and experience are unparalleled, and we are confident that his presence will inspire our athletes to strive for excellence both on and off the court."

Anderson expressed enthusiasm about the opportunity: "I am honored to be a part of this fantastic program. I look forward to working with the talented players and dedicated staff here. My goal is to share my experiences and knowledge to help these young athletes achieve their dreams."

With Anderson on board, Florida Coastal Prep is positioned to enhance its training programs and provide greater opportunities for athletes. His mentorship will be central to developing well-rounded individuals who excel in both academics and athletics.
