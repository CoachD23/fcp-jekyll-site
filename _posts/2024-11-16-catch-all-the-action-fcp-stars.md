November is packed at Florida Coastal Prep — from game action you can watch live to player recognition and some major program announcements. Here's everything you need to stay connected with the Spartans.

## Watch Every Moment: How to Catch FCP Games Live

Never miss a game. Florida Coastal Prep streams every matchup so fans, families, and coaches can follow the action from anywhere.

**YouTube Channel** — All FCP games are archived with organized playlists so you can catch replays and highlight cuts any time. [Subscribe to the FCP YouTube channel](#) to get notified every time we go live.

**VEO LIVE App** — For real-time notifications and instant access during live games, download the VEO LIVE app. Set up alerts for FCP so you're the first to know when tip-off hits.

Whether you're watching from across the country or just want to review film, both platforms give you full access to the Spartans in action.

## Player Highlights: FCP Superstars of the Month

Every month we recognize the Spartans who set the standard — not just with their game, but with their attitude and leadership.

### Leader of the Month: Relja Djordjevic

Relja Djordjevic earned this month's leadership recognition for consistently setting the tone the right way — always on time, always prepared, always pushing teammates to raise their level. Leadership at FCP isn't just about scoring; it's about how you show up every single day. Relja shows up.

### Teammate of the Month: Bud Davis

Bud Davis was named Teammate of the Month for embodying everything we stand for at FCP. The ultimate team player, Bud brings energy, unselfishness, and a winning attitude that lifts the entire program. Players like Bud make the Spartans better than the sum of their parts.

## Special Guest Alert: ESPN's Paul Biancardi Visits FCP

ESPN National Recruiting Director **Paul Biancardi** visited Florida Coastal Prep for a sessions with our Post Grad players. Biancardi — one of the most respected evaluators in college basketball — watched our athletes up close, sharing recruiting insights and giving players direct feedback on what D1 coaches are looking for.

This marks one of the most significant third-party validations a prep program can receive. Having an ESPN analyst on campus evaluating your players sends a clear message to college coaches across the country.

This isn't a highlight reel. It's the full story of what it takes to compete at this level and what happens when you commit to the process.
